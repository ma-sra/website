We Protect Us

The Socialist Rifle Association's mission is to foster strength and resilience in the face of reaction and collapse. We exist to create infrastructure for our communities to challenge and eventually make obsolete the structures of oppression that run our daily lives. Our vision of the collective liberation of all people is best summed up by our motto: We Protect Us. It is a mindset which does not look to the police to protect us, to the landlords to house us, or to the bosses to feed us. Instead, we look to each other.

"Under no pretext should arms and ammunition be surrendered; any attempt to disarm the workers must be frustrated, by force if necessary."
~Karl Marx
