self-defense space is overwhelmingly dominated by exclusionary right-wing perspectives. Our chapter seeks to fight back against this tendency by building an inclusive and safe space to discuss our shared interest in firearms and self-defense. Members regularly discuss and review products, share info on the safety of local ranges and FFLs for marginalized folks, and meet up to go to the range together.
